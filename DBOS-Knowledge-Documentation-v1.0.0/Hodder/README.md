# Hodder

## Overview

Hodder contains unit-based learning and reference resources for leadership and management in adult care.

## Purpose

Provide structured source material for Level 5 adult care learning, teaching, planning and knowledge development.

## Current Content

The folder contains unit resources covering areas such as:

- leadership and management;
- governance and regulatory processes;
- decision-making;
- business and resource management;
- team leadership;
- workforce learning and professional development;
- supervision;
- safeguarding;
- mental capacity and restrictive practice;
- partnership working;
- leading change and communication;
- organisational vision.

## Use This Package When

- preparing teaching and learning;
- supporting Level 5 knowledge development;
- building learner resources;
- checking topic coverage;
- planning professional discussions or development activities.

## Important Use Rule

These resources support teaching and knowledge development. They do not replace the applicable awarding-organisation criteria, apprenticeship standard, current legislation or formal assessment instructions.

## Management Rules

- Preserve original source files.
- Keep unit numbers and titles visible in file names.
- Add new editions as controlled updates.
- Do not silently replace older source material where historical comparison is required.
