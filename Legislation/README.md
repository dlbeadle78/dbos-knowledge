# Legislation

## Overview

Legislation provides a maintained index of current legislation, regulations and authoritative guidance relevant to adult social care.

## Purpose

Direct DBOS users to live official sources rather than relying on static PDF copies that may become outdated.

## Source Approach

The preferred sources are:

- legislation.gov.uk
- GOV.UK
- Health and Safety Executive
- Care Quality Commission
- Care Inspectorate Wales
- Welsh Government
- Information Commissioner's Office
- NICE
- Skills for Care
- Social Care Wales

## Current Coverage

The legislation index includes:

- adult social care law;
- CQC and Welsh regulatory requirements;
- mental capacity and human rights;
- safeguarding;
- LOLER;
- PUWER;
- RIDDOR;
- COSHH;
- manual handling;
- fire safety;
- infection prevention and control;
- medicines;
- food safety;
- data protection;
- employment;
- complaints and duty of candour;
- emergency planning.

## Use Rules

- Check jurisdiction before applying a source.
- Distinguish England and Wales requirements.
- Check commencement, amendments and outstanding changes.
- Use live guidance pages where guidance is regularly updated.
- Keep dated local copies only where required for audit, qualification or historical evidence.
- Review links periodically.
