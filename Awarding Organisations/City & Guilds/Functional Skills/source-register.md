## Version-control requirement

Each indexed document must record:

- document title;
- subject;
- level;
- qualification number or assessment code where applicable;
- version number;
- publication or update date;
- status;
- official URL;
- date checked;
- replacement or supersession notes.

DBOS must use the version that applies to the learner's registration, assessment window and centre requirements. The latest document must not automatically be applied to historic evidence.

## Retrieval safeguards

- Keep English and Mathematics content separate.
- Distinguish Level 1 from Level 2 requirements.
- Quote criteria and subject content accurately when exact wording matters.
- Identify the handbook or guidance version used.
- Do not treat chief examiner commentary as a replacement for the qualification handbook or statutory subject content.
- Recheck the official source before assessment, compliance or centre-delivery decisions.
