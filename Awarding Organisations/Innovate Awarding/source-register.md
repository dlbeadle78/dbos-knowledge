# Innovate Awarding — Source Register

## Publisher

Innovate Awarding Limited

## Official source

https://innovateawarding.org

## Authority classification

Awarding organisation and end-point assessment organisation. Official publisher material is the authoritative source for its qualifications, apprenticeship assessment information, specifications and support materials.

## Approved DBOS scope

DBOS may retrieve and use verified Innovate Awarding information relating to:

- apprenticeship provision at Levels 2, 3, 4 and 5;
- current, future, superseded and closed versions of apprenticeship products;
- standard references and version numbers;
- knowledge, skills and behaviours where published within the applicable official material;
- diploma and qualification specifications;
- units, learning outcomes, assessment criteria and qualification numbers;
- end-point assessment requirements, methods, guidance and support material;
- first-start, operational, withdrawal and closure information where published;
- links to the corresponding official documents and overview pages.

## Version-control requirement

Innovate Awarding may publish multiple versions of the same apprenticeship or qualification. Each indexed record must therefore include, where available:

- product title;
- level;
- apprenticeship standard reference;
- qualification or product number;
- version number;
- effective or first-start date;
- end, withdrawal or closure date;
- status;
- official overview URL;
- official specification or support-document URL;
- date checked;
- notes on replacement or relationship to another version.

DBOS must not assume that the newest version applies to every learner. The applicable version must be selected from the learner's governing dates and programme information.

## Current discovery source

The Innovate Awarding assessment catalogue currently lists qualifications and apprenticeship products across multiple sectors and levels, including distinct versions of the same standard. This catalogue is the starting point for building the detailed version index.

Catalogue:

https://innovateawarding.org/services/assessment/

## Retrieval safeguards

- Use exact official wording when reporting KSBs, units or criteria.
- Identify the source document and version used.
- Do not merge requirements from different versions.
- Flag missing dates or ambiguous applicability for professional confirmation.
- Recheck the official source before high-stakes assessment or compliance decisions.
