# Leader in Adult Care Level 5 — EPA Methods and Grading

**Standard:** ST0008  
**Versions indexed:** 1.0, 1.1 and 1.2  
**Checked:** 22 July 2026

## Direct answer

Across the three publicly listed Innovate Awarding versions, the two end-point assessment methods are:

1. **Observation of Leadership** — 90 minutes in total, comprising a 60-minute observation followed by 30 minutes of post-observation questioning. Innovate states that this may take place at the apprentice's workplace or remotely.
2. **Professional Discussion** — 90 minutes, with a PC or laptop and Wi-Fi connectivity required for remote delivery.

The published overall grading scale is:

- Fail
- Pass
- Distinction

## Gateway requirements shown by Innovate

- Level 5 Diploma in Leadership and Management for Adult Care
- Level 2 English and Maths, if applicable

## Important evidence boundary

The public Innovate overview pages confirm the assessment method names, timings, gateway requirements, overall grading scale and resit prices. They do **not** set out the complete pass and distinction descriptors or the detailed KSB-to-method mapping.

DBOS must therefore not infer detailed grading rules from the overall `Fail/Pass/Distinction` label. Those rules should be ingested separately from the applicable official assessment plan or authorised Innovate support material in Phase 2.
